Cheng-Hao Chen (Ryan Chen)

I am an M.S. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University based in West Lafayette, IN.

I am in the Intelligent Imaging Lab at Purdue University, advised by Professor Stanley Chan.

My research centers on efficient image restoration, with a primary focus on optimizing High Dynamic Range (HDR) denoising pipelines. I leverage techniques such as knowledge distillation to streamline complex models for highly efficient inference. My previous work also explores Generative AI, utilizing diffusion models for image synthesis and computational photography.
